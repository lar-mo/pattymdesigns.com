About the Floral Shop

Yes, I live here. My home is my studio and flower shop where I design and create custom arrangements for you and for your special occasions. I welcome all inquiries with a smile.

I buy my plant materials from suppliers and from local stores when I see beautiful foliage and flowers that I can't resist.

Delivery and pick up
I am a small part-time and local shop so I only take orders within Portland.

No charge if you pick up from my shop. There is a $10 delivery charge if I deliver within 5 miles from my shop.

I will need to use a delivery service outside of my five mile limit and that charge will be passed on to you.
